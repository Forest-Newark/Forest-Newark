About Me

I am currently a senior software engineer working at Fidelity Investments where I provide technical leadership for the content delivery API for Fidelity.com. This includes API design, implementation, testing, maintenance, and support of a multi-component AWS-based architecture.

I began my career as a Teacher in the Durham public school system, where I created the Music Technology and Audio Production program at the School for Creative Studies allowing students to use technology to unlock and share their creativity. After four years in front of the classroom, I decided to pursue my passion for computer programming and enrolled in the Java Engineering Program at the Iron Yard, Raleigh. Upon completion of the Iron Yard program, I joined Fidelity Investments as a full-time software engineer and have been a member of many teams across the organization.

I continue to serve as a soldier in the South Carolina Army National Guard Band, where I participate as a guitar player in the rockband as well as assisting with light and sound production for our concerts.

I am excited for any opportunity to share my passion for technology, problem-solving, and collaboration.
