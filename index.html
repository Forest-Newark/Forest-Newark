About Me

I am a software developer, teacher, musician, and Army soldier based in Durham, North Carolina. I studied music education at Furman University in Greenvile, SC. After completing my undergraduate degree, I created the Music Technology and Audio Production program at the School for Creative Studies allowing students use technology to unlock and share their creativity. After four years in front of the classroom I decided to pursue my passion for computer programming and enrolled in the Java Engineering Program at the Iron Yard, Raleigh. Upon complition of the Iron Yard program I joined Fidelty Investments as a full time software engineer. I am excited for any opportunity to share my passion for technology, problem-solving, and collaboration.
